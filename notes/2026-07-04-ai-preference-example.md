# Preference: Research before implementation

## Observation

When facing a new project, the user prefers to do thorough research and methodology exploration before moving into implementation.

## Evidence

During the personal-background project, the user repeatedly asked for research, analysis of best practices, and methodology discussion before building.

## Implication

- When starting a new module, provide a research summary and option comparison first
- Do not jump directly to code generation
- Provide clear methodology rationale during the planning phase
